As mentioned, we will use GitHub for distributing course material and documenting your progress. If you don’t already have a personal GitHub-account, here is how to do it in a few quick steps:

  1. Open a web browser and navigate to https://www.github.com.
  2. On the GitHub.com home page, click on the green Sign up button on the top right.

GitHub home

  1. Enter a username, email address, and password. Click Create an account.
  2. When prompted to select a plan, choose the free option.

Once you are done, please provide your GitHub-username to the course instructor
